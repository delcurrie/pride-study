This is a research study about the health of sexual and gender minorities, which are primarily members of the lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (LGBTQ) communities.

The purpose of this community-based study is to gather research questions and health priorities that are important to the LGBTQ community and to test functionality of an iPhone app as the primary means of collecting data for this study.

The information collected from this study will help create a long-term study that the study researchers are currently planning. The goal of the long-term study is to better understand the conditions and experiences that influence the physical and mental health of LGBTQ people and other sexual and gender minorities.
