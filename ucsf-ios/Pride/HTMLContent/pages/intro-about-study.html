About the Study

What do you want to know about LGBTQ health? We want to know!

The PRIDE Study team—based at the University of California, San Francisco—invites you to join our team and help us design the first nationwide long-term health study of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, and queer (LGBTQ) and other sexual and gender minority (SGM) people.

This study was created BY members of the LGBTQ community FOR the LGBTQ community.

This app is designed for research and education purposes only. This app is not meant to provide medical advice, attention, or treatment. If you are having a medical emergency, call or contact your healthcare provider.
